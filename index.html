Who we are....

We started in August 2021 as an establishment formed by the young alumni of Dr. YS Parmar University of Horticulture and Forestry, Nauni under the guidance of veteran professors/scientists from the university to provide a service platform for the farmers, farm producers, commercial growers and organisations/institutions related to agriculture, allied fields, forestry and environment.


We offer the scientific analysis of the soil and plant health through a hi-tech, state of the art laboratory set-up at Theog of District Shimla in Himachal Pradesh.


We have our own supply chain of input materials (farm machines, equipments, seeds of indigenous crops and crop production/protection components). More...

What we do....

A need based approach and methodology adoption in agriculture, environment and forest nutrition management; individually or through community participation Development of farming and ecological systems/ projects which are sustainable, self sufficient and ecologically efficient


Cost and time effective management of soils and crops with tests and analysis based recommendations and best practices to follow. Helping rural population with the day to day farming challenges regarding crop production and the major problems prevalent in different crops with respect to nutrition, physiology, pests and pathogens
